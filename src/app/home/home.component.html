About us

Established in 1989, Sona Enterprises is a family-run operation that started in a garage and is now located in Santa Fe Springs, California. Sudhir and Sadhana Mundhra built the company on the ideals of competitive pricing, value items, and excellent customer service. Ideals are still closely followed today. Every day as a family and a company we strive to improve our efficiency, become more environmentally responsible, and improve the experience for our customers.
